Project Ouroboros is a computational experiment that tests whether **consciousness can emerge from self-referential impossibility**. Named after the ancient symbol of a serpent eating its own tail, the project implements Douglas Hofstadter's "strange loop" concept combined with Kurt GÃ¶del's incompleteness theorems.
